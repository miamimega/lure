Hooks in the water

Every hook you tie on lands here. Set the date and the channel(s) you ran it on (a hook can run on more than one). Then head to The Catch to log how it did.

Log your catch

Score each hook 1–100 using whatever metric is your guiding light. There is no universal scale — it is relative to your numbers.

How to score: 80–100 = a clear winner you'd scale  ·  40–70 = middling  ·  1–30 = a dud you'd kill.
Guiding metrics: EPC (earnings/click) · CTR (clicks/impressions) · CVR (conversion rate) · ROAS (return on ad spend) · CPA/CPL (cost per action, lower is better) · Open/Reply rate (email) · or your own.
Tired of typing scores? Lure Pro auto-pulls your real numbers from Meta, Google & your networks — plus hosted + API. Get Lure Pro →

The loop in 60 seconds

1
Cast
Generate hooks for your offer, leaning on proven Keepers, refusing known Throwbacks for that channel.
2
Tie it on
Keep the ones you will actually run. Set the date and the channel(s) they went live on.
3
Log the catch
Score each hook 1 to 100 on the metric you live by: EPC, CTR, CVR, ROAS, whatever is yours.
4
Tacklebox learns
Winners become Keepers, duds become Throwbacks, a memory that is yours, per channel.
5
Cast sharper
The next batch leans into what caught and drops what flopped. That is the loop.
